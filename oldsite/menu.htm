Cafe Deluxe serves up a wide variety of food and drink in a bustling, airy, bistro-style atmosphere.  The menu is a rich mixture of traditional comfort foods, such as Grilled Meatloaf with Creole Sauce and Chicken Pot Pie, and more innovative dishes, such as Pan Roasted Halibut with Corn and Asparagus Saute, Penne Pasta with Chicken and Asparagus, and a Grilled Salmon Nicoise Salad.  The wine list is well-chosen and makes an excellent compliment to the eclectic menu.

With its leather booths, mahogany bar and over-sized mirrors, Cafe Deluxe combines the charm of a neighborhood tavern with the sparkle and energy of a bustling European bistro.  The service is nothing less than superb, all the while maintaining a white-tablecloth atmosphere without the white-tablecloth attitude or price.
