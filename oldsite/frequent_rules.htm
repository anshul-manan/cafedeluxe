  • Earn points and redeem rewards when dining at Cafe Deluxe and Tortilla Coast.
  • Receive one point for each dollar you spend on food & beverages (excluding tax and tip).
  • Points are not awarded for the purchase of gift cards, catering or private dining events.
  • Points are credited and redeemed on your account when presenting your Frequent Dining card (along with your form of payment) to your server at the restaurant.
  • Only the member paying the bill may receive points. If a bill is paid by credit card, the name on the credit card must match the name on the Dining card account.
  • Members cannot get credit for visits prior to enrollment in the Program. 
  • Points are non-transferable.
  • Points have no cash value and may not be redeemed for cash or applied to tax or gratuities.
  • The program is intended for individual use only. Spouses may not share membership. Points may not be accumulated for a business entity.
  • The restaurant reserves the right to audit any account at any time.  Accounts will be deactivated, if fraud is suspected.
  • Federal and State tax liabilities are the responsibility of the cardholder. Laws may prohibit accumulation of points from alcohol purchases only.
  • Any errors on guest check receipt must be adjusted on the day the transaction was made.
  • Employees of Cafe Deluxe and Tortilla Coast - and including their family members or people living in the same household as an employee - are not eligible to participate.
  • Members must be 18 years of age or older.
  • Accounts will be deactivated if not used within 12 months, and any unused points will be forfeited.
  • The restaurant may cancel this program or modify its terms at any time.
  • Accumulated points are not member property and may be revoked, cancelled, limited, or modified at any time, even though such action may affect the member’s right to use previously accumulated points.
  • Card value will not be replaced if card is lost, stolen, or used without your permission.
  • From time to time, you will receive email from the restaurants regarding the program and / or special promotions.
  • When points are redeemed, you must present your card and your account balance is reduced accordingly.
  • Use of the dining program card constitutes acceptance of all program terms.
