Thank you for registering for the 7th Clean Coal Technology Conference.

Thank you for submitting your registration form for the 7th Clean Coal Technology Conference. You have been added to the Conference registration list.  In order to guarantee your registration, a registration fee of $400 must be received no later than June 7, 1999.

Payment of the registration fee may be made by checks/bank drafts or money orders payable to the National Mining Association (NMA).  Checks or bank drafts must be drawn on a U.S. bank or a foreign bank with a New York branch.  Credit purchase orders are also accepted.  Credit Cards are NOT accepted.

You may mail your registration fee to:

The Center for Conference Management
U.S. Department of Energy
1000 Independence Avenue, SW
Washington, DC  20585

If you have any problems or questions, please call 202-586-7920.
