Cafe Deluxe and Tortilla Coast are proud to support their neighborhood communities and charities. Please follow these guidelines in order for us to consider your charity. Charity requests are handled by our outside office (not the restaurant staff).

You may FAX or MAIL your request. Phone requests are not accepted.

Fax:  202.364.0177
Mail: 3228 Wisconsin Avenue, Washington DC 20015

Lead time - 14 days.

Due to the large number of requests we receive (more than twenty per week), we're sorry that we cannot support every cause and we can not respond to those that we decline. However, you are welcome to follow-up via email Charity@CafeDeluxe.com and we can advise you of status of your request.
