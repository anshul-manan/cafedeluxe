NOVEMBER NEWS

Happy Holidays from Cafe Deluxe!

We've been busy at Cafe Deluxe! We have spent the last several months speaking with many of you, our frequent dining members, about what you love about our restaurants and how we can make your experiences even better. We are acting on many of your great suggestions, including more regular communication. With that, we are launching our new monthly newsletter designed to bring you our latest and greatest news and offer some special treats. Read and enjoy!

Cafe Deluxe Gaithersburg Coming Soon!

We have been working hard on our newest Cafe Deluxe in Gaithersburg, Md. Located in the RIO Entertainment Center with great waterfront views, this will be Cafe Deluxe's fourth restaurant in the DC area. We are very excited to bring our delicious food, great drinks and genuine service to the Gaithersburg community. Stay tuned in Mid-November for news about our opening!

Join us for our New Happy Hour

We are pleased to introduce our new happy hour menu. Monday through Friday, 3:30pm-7:00pm, enjoy a wide range of draft beers for $4 and house wines for $4 a glass. We also offer a great selection of bar bites including Mini Gruyere Grilled Cheese with Prosciutto, Short Rib Spring Rolls, and Prosciutto and Mozzarella Stuffed Peppadews.

Brunch Just Got Easier

Based on popular demand, we are now open for brunch an hour earlier! Starting at 10:00 am on Saturday and Sundays at all locations, enjoy Cafe Deluxe's famous selections including our legendary Eggs Benedict and delicious Bloody Marys!
